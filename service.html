services

PT. Wahana Pamunah Limbah Industri gives following service :

  • Hazardous waste management
  • Treatment of disposal hazardous waste
  • Recycling and recovery of economical materials
  • Hazardous waste transporter
  • Secure and controlled temporary storage of hazardous waste
  • Analyzing laboratory education and training center for hazardous waste management and emergency responding system.

In the 21 st century, the need for waste treatment is increasingly important than ever. In Indonesia alone, most of local and industrial wastes are uncontrolled, dumped in the community open spaces. This leads to higher pollution, greater environmental damage, and decrease community’s health status. Now is the time to go beyond our present situation and look forward: not only these dumps financially and environmentally unfriendly, they put our future into jeopardy. WPLI Provides the solution for you. We offer :

  • Highest level of technology imported from Korea, Japan and United States to treat the waste, following the rules thoroughly.
  • Most proficient management team: our managing staff is on site 24/7 during the treatment processes to ensure that every step is conducted in an accurate way.
  • Regularized system inspections: in accordance with ISO 9001 and ISO 14000 standards, WPLI performs system check-ups daily, weekly, and monthly to ensure the best quality of service.
  • Transportation services: WPLI offers transportation service for collection and pickup of wastes from each company, tailored to one’s needs. Equipments are always ready at your needs.

Transporting Waste

Wahana Pamunah Wimbah Industri (PT WPLI) is a company engaged in the processing of hazardous and toxic waste materials. PT. WPLI may undertake the transport of hazardous and toxic waste materials according to the permit from the Ministry of Environment and Forestry as well as the Ministry of Transportation Department in the form of environmental permit, Amdal, RKL, RPL, transportation permit, operating permit of hazardous materials and hazardous (incinerator and evaporator) and permits waste utilization Hazardous and toxic materials. Permits owned by PT. WPLI is as follows:

  • Environmental Permit, Amdal, RKL & RPL namely:
    1. SK MenLH No. 285, dated August 9, 2006 on the environmental feasibility of industrial activities of hazardous and toxic waste materials management in Parakan Village, Jawilan Sub-district, Serang District.
    2. SK MenLH N0.463, dated August 27, 2009 on environmental management plan (RKL) and environmental monitoring plan (RPL) additional industrial development activity of hazardous and toxic waste management in Parakan Village, Jawilan District, Serang Regency.
    3. SK MenLHK No.011.1.01 Year 2015 Concerning the environmental feasibility of the development activity plan for the utilization of hazardous and toxic waste into Briquette Iron in Parakan Village, Jawilan District, Serang Regency, Banten Province by PT. WPLI.
  • Transportation Permit:

    • SK MenLH No. S.1133 / VPLB3 / PPLB3 / PLB.3 / 11/2017, dated 29 November 2017 on recommendations for the transport of hazardous and toxic waste materials PT. Wahana Pamunah Waste Industries.

  • Waste Treatment Permits Hazardous and toxic Materials:

    • SK MenLH No. 77 of 2011, dated 26 April 2011 on Operation Permit for processing equipment (Incinerator) of Hazardous and Toxic Waste PT. Wahana Pamunah Waste Industries.


    Wastes processed in the incinerator-liquid evaporator and solid, among others:

    1. Expired solid materials or reject and filter cake, sludge WWTP, degradable waste, gradable and hospital waste.
    2. Liquid waste Hazardous and toxic Substances Organic Hydrocarbon Persistent.
  • Permission for the Use of Hazardous and Toxic Wastes:

    • SK MenLH No. 149 Year 2012, dated June 29, 2012 on Permit Utilization of Hazardous and Toxic Waste PT. WPLI Wastes that will be used are fly ash, bottom ash, carbide-residue, EAF dust, sand foundry and cupola dust.
